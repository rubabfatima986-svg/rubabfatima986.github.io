title: **"Foreign Aid & Female Education in Developing Countries"**

## Overview
This study examines how foreign aid composition affects female 
education outcomes across 52 developing countries including LDCs, 
LMICs, and MICs, using fixed-effects econometric models in Stata.

## Key Themes
- Aid modality and female education outcomes
- Cross-country analysis across 52 developing countries
- Sectoral aid allocation and gender equity
- Policy implications for development finance

## Key Findings
- Aid composition significantly affects female education outcomes
- Sector-specific aid outperforms general budget support
- Effects vary across income groups and fragile states

## Tools & Methods
**Tools:** Power BI · Stata  
**Methods:** Fixed Effects · Panel Econometrics  
**Data:** 52-country panel dataset
