I am a development researcher and policy professional with over two 
years of experience across multilateral organisations, UN agencies, 
and policy institutes, including the **Green Climate Fund (GCF)** and 
**UNDP**. I specialise in climate finance, gender equity, aid 
effectiveness, and development economics, with a strong track record 
of producing high-quality analytical outputs for policy use.

I hold a fully funded **Master of Development Policy (Dean's List)** 
from KDI School of Public Policy and Management, South Korea, and a 
**Bachelor of Science in Economics** from Quaid-i-Azam University, 
Pakistan. My master's research built a 52-country panel dataset to 
examine how aid modality affects female education outcomes using 
fixed-effects econometric models.
